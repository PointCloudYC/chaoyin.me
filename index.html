I am an Associate Researcher at the Geography Institute of Guangzhou, Guangdong Academy of Sciences. I received my Ph.D. from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2022. My research interests include 3D point cloud processing, deep learning, multi-modal AI (foundation models, 2D/3D LLMs), weakly-supervised and long-tailed learning, and remote sensing applications. I have published 3 first-author Q1 papers in Automation in Construction and received the Best Paper Award at ICCBEI 2019. I serve as PI for research grants totaling 680K RMB from the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation and the Guangdong Province Overseas Postdoctoral Talent Program.
